# Source: NeoWallet GenZ Launch Brief

- **Type:** brief
- **Date:** 2026-02-10
- **Participants:** Sarah Chen (Product Lead), Mike Ross (CEO)
- **Context:** Initial vision document for our new fintech product targeting the Gen Z market.

---

## Vision
NeoWallet aims to be the first "social banking" experience for Gen Z. Traditional banks are boring and solitary. We want to make finance viral, multiplayer, and deeply integrated with social status.

## Core Value Proposition
1. **Multiplayer Finance**: Shared pots, public transaction feeds (venmo style but richer), and "squad goals" for saving.
2. **AI-Driven Coaching**: An aggressive AI personality that roasts you when you spend too much on boba.
3. **Mobile-Only**: We are not building a web app. This is 100% iOS/Android. If it's not in your pocket, it doesn't exist.

## Business Model
We will charge a flat $5/month subscription. Our research shows Gen Z hates ads and hidden fees. They value transparency and are willing to pay for premium "status" features like metal cards and profile skins.

## Timeline
MVP in 3 months. Launch usage of the new "Stark" banking API for instant settlement.
