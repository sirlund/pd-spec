# Source: Competitor Landscape 2025

- **Type:** workshop
- **Date:** 2025-11-20
- **Participants:** Market Research Team
- **Context:** Analysis of top 3 competitors in the region.

---

## 1. CashFlow Z
- **Model:** Freemium. Core features free, expensive skins are paid.
- **Vibe:** Very social. Public feeds are default.
- **Traction:** Massive. 10M users.

## 2. SaveMate
- **Model:** $10/month.
- **Vibe:** Serious, privacy-focused. "Bank for adults".
- **Traction:** Struggling. Losing users to CashFlow Z.

## 3. FinTok
- **Model:** Ad-supported.
- **Features:** Video-based financial advice.
- **Insight:** Users tolerate ads if they are entertaining.

## Summary
The market is bifurcating. Free/Social apps are winning volume. Paid/Privacy apps are niche but high ARPU. There is NO successful app that charges a subscription AND pushes social features. It's one or the other.
