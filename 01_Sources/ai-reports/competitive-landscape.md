## Market Overview

The mining technology software market is estimated at $4.2B globally, with a CAGR of 8.3% through 2030. Key segments include fleet management systems (FMS), mine planning, and operational intelligence platforms.

## Key Competitors

### Hexagon Mining (HxGN MineOperate)
- Market leader in fleet management with ~35% market share in open-pit operations
- Strengths: deep OEM integration with Caterpillar, real-time positioning accuracy
- Weaknesses: UX is dated, requires extensive training (typically 40+ hours for operators)
- Pricing: enterprise license model, $500K-$2M per site deployment

### Wenco International Mining Systems
- Strong in mid-market operations, known for flexibility
- Strengths: modular deployment, works with mixed fleets
- Weaknesses: limited 3D visualization capabilities, no AI/ML features
- Pricing: subscription model, ~$150K-$400K annually

### Maptek (Vulcan + BlastLogic)
- Dominant in geological modeling and blast design
- Strengths: industry-standard for mine planning, strong technical community
- Weaknesses: siloed products, poor real-time operational integration
- Pricing: per-seat licensing, $30K-$80K per seat
