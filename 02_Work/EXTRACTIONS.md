## [01_Sources/TIMining_8-Layer_Diagram.pptx]
- Type: Presentation – 8-Layer Architecture Diagram
- Date: unknown
- Preprocessed: yes
- Extracted: 2026-03-08T03:34

### Raw Claims
1. "TIMining Platform uses an 8-layer interconnection architecture with upward data flow and downward prescriptions & control."
2. "Layer 1 (Data Origination & Integration): uses neutral connectors to FMS systems (Modular, Hexagon, Wenco), planning suites, block model, GPS, drill & blast, and plant signals."
3. "Layer 2 (Real-Time Spatial Reconstruction): computational geometry & soft-sensor inference provides pit surface tracking, drill progress monitoring, blast inventory status, and dump identification; powered by 'Aware' core algorithms."
4. "Layer 3 (Operational State Modeling): discrete-event models (Orchestra) with process math for load-haul dynamics, queues, and interference; inputs from Aris/Tangram/SICT systems."
5. "Layer 4 (Plan Context & Spatial Adherence): Delta2025 tracks design intent vs execution with current-time adherence monitoring, polygon-vs-surface reconciliation, and volumetrics analysis."
6. "Layer 5 (Eventing, Controls & Governance): data QA/QC, normalization, time-space indexing, RBAC, audit trails, standardized APIs, and deployment backplane."
7. "Layer 6 (Situational Awareness & UX): role-specific real-time views & dashboards ('Aware') for supervisors, dispatch, planners, and managers; supports mobile & desktop access."
8. "Layer 7 (Analytics, Anomalies & Auto-Projections): soft-sensor monitoring, performance-to-plan KPIs, and automatic short-horizon simulations via Orchestra."
9. "Layer 8 (AI Copilot & Autonomy Enablement): Project TIM delivers natural-language diagnostics, prescriptive actions; autonomy-ready geometry feeds to robots/dispatch systems."
10. "The architecture enables machine autonomy at mining scale through hierarchical data integration, spatial modeling, contextual awareness, and autonomous prescriptions."
